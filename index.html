Welcome

Dr. Arash Karimi Pour earned a Doctoral degree in Civil Engineering from the University of Texas at El Paso (UTEP) in 2023. He serves as a senior researcher with the Innovative Structural Engineering and Mechanics Group, where he focuses on advanced materials, structural performance, and computational analysis. Dr. Karimi Pour spent four years as a Research and Teaching Assistant in the Department of Civil Engineering at UTEP, contributing to major research projects. He has been affiliated with UTEP since 2019 and was a standing member of the Center for Transportation Infrastructure Systems (CTIS) from 2019 to 2020.

Dr. Karimi Pour completed his graduate studies in Structural Engineering at Ferdowsi University of Mashhad (FUM) and brings more than a decade of experience as a senior civil engineer and researcher. His expertise includes finite element methods, retrofitting of concrete members, sustainable construction materials, and advanced computational modeling. His current research focuses on improving the structural performance of sustainable materials, computational mechanics, constitutive modeling of civil engineering materials (including soils, concrete, and asphalt), advanced materials and devices, and the behavior of materials under extreme environments. He also serves as an editor for several scientific journals and as a reviewer for leading organizations including Elsevier, Wiley, ASCE, and Springer.
